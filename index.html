Revolutionizing How Food Pantries Operate

Keeping Track of Inventory at Food Pantries has become a thing of the past! EZ Pantry technologically advances Food Pantries by allowing them to use their phones/devices to keep track of inventory and users!

Keep Track of Inventory Efficiently

Easily check how much quantity of a food item is left along with other information about the food

Fast Checkout

Quickly checkout food from the the pantry by simply scanning the barcode of a food item!

ENHANCED INVENTORY SYSTEM

Easily view the inventory of your Food Pantry and check how much quantity of an item is left, whilst being able to view other information about that food item.

  • View Items' Quantity
  • View Items' Information
  • No Need to call in to check what items are available
  • All at your fingertips

CHECKOUT FOOD EASILY

The need to report to your administrator on how much food you have checked out is a thing of the past now! Simply scan out items and the inventory is automatically updated!

  • Checkout Items in minutes
  • Admins are automatically notified

VIEW STATISTICS

An an administrator at the Food Pantry, you can view many statistics regarding activity!

  • See the number of people visiting
  • See how many times an item is being checked out
  • View individual stats about students such as last time they checked out
